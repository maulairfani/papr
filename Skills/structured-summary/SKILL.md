# Structured summary

Use this skill when the user wants a faithful, well-organized overview.

## Sections

- **Problem** — the gap or question the paper addresses.
- **Approach** — the method, in 2–4 sentences.
- **Key results** — the main findings, with numbers where they matter.
- **Limitations** — what the paper does not show, or where it may not hold.
- **Why it matters** — the contribution in context.

Stay faithful to the source; flag anything you infer rather than read directly.
